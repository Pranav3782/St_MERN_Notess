About University


McGill University is a public English-language research university located in Montreal, Quebec, Canada, founded in 1821 by a royal charter. It was established using a bequest from James McGill, a prominent Montreal merchant, and was originally named McGill College before officially becoming McGill University in 1885. The university is consistently ranked as one of the top universities in Canada and globally, currently holding the 45th position in the World University Rankings 2025. It is recognized as Canada's top medical-doctoral university
